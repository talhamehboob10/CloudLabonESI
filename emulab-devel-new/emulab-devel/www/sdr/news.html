Jun 29 2005: SDR Wiki

The SDR Wiki is ready to go! We expect the amount of information in the Wiki to grow rapidly, now that the system is up and running.

In case you don't already know, a Wiki is a Web-based collaboration environment. The most important difference between a "normal" Web page and a Wiki page is that you can edit a Wiki page. Wiki helps you write easy-to-read pages through the use of lightweight markup notations. The "source code" for a Wiki page looks a lot like normal text instead of clunky HTML.

Our Wiki system has a lot of built-in help, pointers to tutorials, and so on. The SDR Wiki is currently open to the public, so if you have something to say about software-defined radio, you can start writing today!
